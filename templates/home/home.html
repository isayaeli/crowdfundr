Who We Are

Access to funding has never been easy to any company, corporate, organizations and even institutions. It is a stumbling block due to most startups being not well informed on existing funding opportunities as well as not knowledgeable on project designing and creative proposal writing to attract funding from different donors. It takes a lot of efforts just to raise your first dollar, this situation is even worse to startups (Business and NGOs) by considering their level of experience and impact reach. It is indeed a nightmare and it has remained a critical challenge getting access to pre-revenue funds for proof-of-concept to early-stage entrepreneurs and NGOs. Most entrepreneurs and small NGOs with viable ideas do not have the required funding to nurture and validate their concepts in the market/ communities. This has led to premature failure of many social enterprises and Small NGOs in Tanzania.

CrowdFundr is a product of Ennovation for change, it is a digital, decentralized crowdfunding platform that enables CrowdFundr verified entrepreneurs and small NGOs to post their empowerment and social enterprise projects and source for impact funding from a network of both corporates (through CSR) and individual donors on CrowdFundr Seed Crowdfunding Platform. SMEs and small NGOs seeking to raise funds from the platform will be verified through; a due diligence process by Ennovation for change and CrowdFundr, go through a capacity building sessions on project designing and proposal writing, then they can upload their projects based on CrowdFundr Standards for their project to be approved to raise funds. Upon completion of the fundraising activity, Ennovation for change will provide post-funding support to the entrepreneurs and small entrepreneurs to ensure that the capital raised is fully used to grow the business or create impacts to beneficiaries, while solving community problems and creating jobs through the business expansion and operation sustainability. Through CrowdFundr, SMEs and small NGOs will be able to access pre-seed capital to prove concepts and grow their enterprise and operations to a commercial and sustainable level.
