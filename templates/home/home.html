Who We Are

We are the platform built to help and making sure that most vulnerable groups in our communities are sustainably raising funds to build and develop their solutions to most pressing community issues, and grow their enterprise and operations from pre-seed capital, prove concepts to commercial and sustainable level.

Projects that are raising funds to create impacts in the communities and improve people`s lives as well as creating a sustainable environment.

Opportunities that have been share on the platform with a direct impacts and support to innovation from our current and future registered innovators.

NGO's from grass root communities with solutions that are targeted to specific groups of undeserved communities with high possibilities of improving their lives.

Donors who are ready to support proof of impact solutions by funding them to create more impacts and scale the reach to more beneficiaries.

How it Works

For SMEs & NGOs

Registration

Users register to be part of the platform and be eligible to upload their projects by filling in their details and are then receiving a demonstration training to write a compelling story to attract donors to donate on their projects.

Fundraising

Users uploads their projects by filling a form and uploading video telling a compelling stories about their projects. Then they get the link to share with their friends, family and their ecosystem to drive traffic to their projects for donation and their projects will be promoted on our social media pages.

Post-Funding Support

The fundraisers will receive mentorship support after they have reached their targets or when their campaign ended in order to make sure that they are making the best use of those raised funds.
